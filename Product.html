Dried mango production method

1. After removing the skin from a mango, cut off the skin
2. Cut the mango with a knife and cut it into a sheet about 0.3-0.5 cm thick.
3. Take a small pot, pour 100 grams of fresh water and 30 grams of sugar, and cook until the sugar is completely dissolved.
4. Then put the sliced mango slices into the cooked sugar in step 3.
5. Let cool and put it in the refrigerator for 3 hours. Carefully remove the mango slices.
6. One by one placed on a drying rack, placed in a cool, ventilated place
7. It is best to turn it once every 3-4 hours during the period
8. Let it dry after about 24 hours
