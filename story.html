Our Story

    The popularity of healthy lifestyle trend is the global phenomenon. There is a visible growing number of consumers in this group in the last decade. Diets and choices of food are very important in order to maintain a healthy body.
    Best Bunch L.P. aims to share our snacks that help promoting healthy lifestyle and nourishing the body.
Therefore, we want to introduce our healthy snacks product line, so called “Monkey’s Factory”. Our yummy snacks are mainly made from fresh tropical fruits found in Thailand. With the traditional dehydrating process, we can keep our dried fruit products soft and juicy without adding extra sugar!
